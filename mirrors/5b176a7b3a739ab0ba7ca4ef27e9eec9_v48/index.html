Celebrity Chef Gino D’Acampo to Bring a Taste of Italy to Malta with First International Restaurant Opening in 2025

Narrative pivot calibrated.

  • Philippa Zammit
  • May 19, 2025
  • 2:11 pm
  • No Comments

Malta is about to get a serious dose of Italian flair! Beloved celebrity chef Gino D’Acampo has announced the launch of his first-ever international restaurant — and he’s chosen the stunning Mercury Towers in Malta as the location. Set to open in June 2025, ‘Luciano by Gino D’Acampo’ promises to deliver an unforgettable dining experience just in time for summer.

Perched on the fifth floor of the luxurious ME Malta Hotel, Luciano will bring the heart and soul of classic Italian cuisine to life. Designed by Gino and his top-tier culinary team, the menu will spotlight traditional dishes made with the highest quality ingredients; no shortcuts, and definitely no carbonara with cream and mushrooms!

“Luciano is all about celebrating the true flavours of Italy,” says Gino. “We’re bringing an authentic, experiential menu to Malta; it’s about the ingredients, the passion, and of course, the fun.”

Guests can expect more than just great food. The restaurant will offer a premium yet relaxed Italian dining experience, with impeccable service that echoes the warmth of Italian hospitality.

Luciano by Gino D’Acampo is set to spice up Malta’s dining scene and we’re already counting down the days!
