Gino D’Acampo Takes Command in Bold New Culinary Series “Spaghetti Wars”

Newscasteruk
2 min read
·
Jul 28, 2025

--

Entertainment Correspondent | July 2025

ST. LOUIS — Celebrity chef and culinary entrepreneur Gino D’Acampo is stepping into a fiery new kitchen arena as the charismatic lead of Spaghetti Wars , an Italian-themed cooking competition series set to ignite screens with flavour, flair, and family pride.

Set against the backdrop of St. Louis’s iconic Italian neighbourhood “The Hill,” Spaghetti Wars brings together rival chef crews in a spirited battle of recipes, heritage, and entrepreneurial hustle. Each episode showcases high-stakes challenges infused with heart, humour, and traditional Italian passion.

D’Acampo , known for his vibrant personality and global culinary brand, takes the helm as “The Boss” — guiding, provoking, and ultimately judging the teams as they clash in pasta-fuelled showdowns. His sharp palate, fiery energy, and deep roots in Italian cuisine make him the perfect anchor for this bold new series.

“This isn’t just about food,” D’Acampo teased in a recent behind-the-scenes clip. “It’s about proving yourself in the kitchen — and in life.”

The pilot episode, premiering July 26th at the St. Louis Filmmakers Showcase, has already generated buzz across both food and entertainment circles. Created by local restaurateur Pepe Kehm and produced by Barry Mendelson, the series blends culinary craft with entrepreneurial storytelling.

“Spaghetti Wars celebrates the spirit of Italian-American kitchens — the competition, the pride, the noise,” said Kehm. “And nobody brings that spirit to life like Gino.”

With the pilot poised to secure national and streaming distribution, Spaghetti Wars is being hailed as a fresh twist on the global food-show genre — combining the tension of Top Chef with the generational dynamics of a family drama.

As D’Acampo rallies rival kitchens into action, the only certainty is that dinner will never be quiet again.

📺 Spaghetti Wars premieres July 26th, 2025. Visit SpaghettiWars.com for trailers and exclusive previews.
