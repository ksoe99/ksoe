The World’s First AI-Powered Digital Memory Firewall

Invented by Simon Lindsay | First Public Disclosure: May 27, 2025

📌 What is EIDETIC™?

EIDETIC (pronounced /aɪˈdɛtɪk/, “eye-DET-ik”) comes from the Greek eidētikos , meaning “pertaining to vivid mental imagery or memory.”

In psychology, eidetic memory is the rare ability to recall images or scenes in extraordinary detail — like a photographic memory.

Today, the internet has that kind of memory — and it remembers everything.

EIDETIC™ , invented by Simon Lindsay, is the first system in history that allows individuals and organizations to shape what the internet remembers — and what it forgets .

⚠️ The Problem

“The internet never forgets.”

Even after headlines fade, scandals resolve, or time passes — old search results, archive links, forum threads, and cached pages continue to echo past reputational threats.

For individuals and brands alike, this results in:

  • Lost jobs, clients, or relationships
  • Misinformation outliving the truth
  • Permanent digital stigmas with no appeal system

Until now, there’s been no structured way to recalibrate digital memory . Suppression was manual, incomplete, and reactive.

✅ The EIDETIC™ Solution

EIDETIC™ is an AI-powered memory firewall that controls how the internet remembers you.

It does this through five core systems:

LayerPurpose🧠 memory_footprints Detects every lingering reference to a person or brand⏳ memory_decay_profiles Scores emotional weight, age, legal status, and public relevance✍️ memory_recalibrators AI-generated counter-content that rewrites outdated narratives📉 eidetic_decay_history Logs how public memory changes over time🧮 generate_decay_score() Dynamically calculates how fast something should fade from visibility

🛠 How It Works

  1. Scan: EIDETIC™ identifies damaging or outdated mentions across Google, forums, archives, and news.
  2. Score: Each memory is analyzed based on:
  • Age
  • Emotional charge
  • Legal resolution
  • Public interest level

4. Schedule: A “memory fadeout plan” is triggered automatically or manually

5. Recalibrate: AI generates counter-narratives, explainer content, or redirection articles

6. Monitor: Visibility is tracked over time; if the threat resurfaces, EIDETIC™ reactivates

🚀 Why It’s Never Been Possible — Until Now

Prior to advanced AI orchestration, no system could:

  • Understand narrative weight and social memory
  • Control search visibility over time
  • Generate relevant, ethical counter-messaging
  • Operate without manual input

EIDETIC™ is the first system to bring all of this together.

It doesn’t just suppress — it forgets responsibly .

🧩 Real-World Use Cases

  • Public Figures: Reduce visibility of resolved controversies
  • Executives & Politicians: Reframe past narratives with updated facts
  • Brands: Quiet long-dead PR issues or viral moments no longer relevant
  • Agencies: Offer lifetime digital equilibrium to clients
